# Camera Movement Prompts

## Prompt 1: Camera movement planning

```text
Design camera movement for this storyboard scene.

Scene:
[describe scene]

Choose from:
- pan
- push-in
- pull-out
- tracking
- orbit
- static close-up

For each movement, explain:
- why it fits the emotional beat
- where it starts and ends
- what the viewer should notice
```

## Prompt 2: Short drama motion guide

```text
Create a camera movement guide for a vertical short drama episode.

Constraints:
- aspect ratio: 9:16
- episode length: [1 / 1.5 / 2 / 3] minutes
- style: [realistic / anime / 3D]
```
